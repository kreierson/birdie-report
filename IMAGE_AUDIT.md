## 🔧 Recommendations

1. **Immediate fixes needed:** Replace the 7 critical images (tennis, muscle car, woman in field, children's art, yoga wall, surprised girl, Pebble Beach for Minnesota). These are embarrassing if anyone notices.

2. **Pexels is the biggest offender.** 6 of 7 critical issues are from Pexels URLs. The Pexels photo IDs appear to have shifted or the search that generated them returned wrong results. **Stop using blind Pexels searches for images.**

3. **For player-specific articles:** Use editorial/wire photos or YouTube thumbnails of the actual player instead of generic stock golf photos.

4. **For product reviews:** Use manufacturer product images (like you already do for Callaway, TaylorMade, Titleist, Scotty Cameron). This is the gold standard.

5. **For course-specific articles:** Use photos that are actually of that course/region. A Minnesota golf article should not show Pebble Beach.

6. **Deduplicate:** Each article should have a unique featured image. Fix the 4 duplicate pairs.

7. **Image validation pipeline:** Before publishing, visually verify every stock photo URL actually shows what you think it shows. Consider adding an automated check that analyzes featured images for relevance.
