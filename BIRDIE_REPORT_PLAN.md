# The Birdie Report - Business Plan & Strategy

## Revenue Goal: $100k/year

### Revenue Streams

**Primary (70% of revenue):**
1. **Affiliate Commissions** ($70k target)
   - Golf equipment (drivers, irons, putters, balls) - 3-8% commission
   - Golf accessories (rangefinders, bags, shoes) - 2-5% commission  
   - Average order value: $150-300
   - Target: 1,200 affiliate sales/month by year 2

**Secondary (30% of revenue):**
2. **Display Advertising** ($20k target)
   - Google AdSense, Media.net
   - Target: 100k pageviews/month for meaningful revenue
3. **Sponsored Content** ($10k target)
   - Equipment manufacturer partnerships (once we have traffic)
   - Course promotion deals
