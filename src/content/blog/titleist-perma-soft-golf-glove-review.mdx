## What Titleist Is Selling Here

Titleist's current **Perma-Soft Men's** page makes a very different promise than the **Players Flex** page.

The pitch is not "tour feel" or "maximum hand-to-grip intimacy" or whatever marketing phrase we want to use to describe expensive glove poetry.

The pitch is:

- **durable cabretta leather**
- **CoolMax mesh** across the knuckles
- dual rows of elastic
- satin reinforcement at the cuff and thumb
- precise sewing and seam placement

That is a much more normal-person glove story.

And normal-person glove stories are useful.

- a glove that makes sense for more rounds and more range sessions
