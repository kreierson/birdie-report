## Buying Friction Is the Real Separator

This is the part golfers skip too often.

The **Opus** is easier to buy because:

- the product line is current
- the pricing is straightforward
- the grind tree is simpler
- you are not dealing with condition buckets and stock gaps

The **Jaws Raw** is harder to buy because:

- the best price may not be tied to the right loft and grind for you
- pre-owned availability can change
- the cheapest option is not automatically the smartest option

That does not mean the **Jaws Raw** is a bad buy.

It means it is a better buy for a golfer who can shop intentionally.
