## Forgiveness and Fit: Different Kinds of Help

This is where the page gets more useful.

The **Ai Smoke MAX** help story is a little more explicit. Callaway leans into the adjustable perimeter weighting and correction lane. It makes sense for golfers who want the driver to acknowledge that their miss pattern still has some chaos in it.

The **Qi35** help story is broader and more neutral. TaylorMade talks about explosive distance, undeniable forgiveness, and more distance from more of the face without making the club feel like a pure rescue product.

So the split is:

- **Ai Smoke MAX** for the golfer who wants help with a capital H
- **Qi35** for the golfer who wants forgiveness without feeling like the driver is making a statement about their problems

That is subtle, but it matters.
