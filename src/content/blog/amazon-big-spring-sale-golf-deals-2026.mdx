## What to Skip

Not everything is worth buying just because it has a sale tag:

- **Clubs from unknown brands** — Amazon is full of random Chinese golf brands that mark up fake MSRPs then "discount" them 40%. If you've never heard of the brand, skip it.
- **Last-gen drivers at 10% off** — A $450 driver at $405 isn't a deal. Check [Callaway Pre-Owned](https://www.amazon.com/s?k=Callaway+Pre-Owned+Golf+Clubs&tag=birdiereport-20) or 2nd Swing for real savings on clubs.
- **Cheap GPS watches** — Anything under $150 for a golf GPS watch is going to disappoint you. Save up for the Garmin.
