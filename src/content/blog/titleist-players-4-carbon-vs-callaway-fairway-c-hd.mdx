Buy the **Callaway Fairway C HD** if you want the smarter bag for most golfers comparing these two exact products.

Buy the **Titleist Players 4 Carbon** if you care enough about bag weight that you are willing to pay premium money for less storage and less flexibility.

That is a much fuller ownership story than the Titleist is trying to tell.

It makes it the more useful golf bag for the broader group of buyers.

## Where the Callaway Gives Ground
