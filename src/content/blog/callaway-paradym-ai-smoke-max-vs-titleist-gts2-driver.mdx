## Why the Callaway Wins for Most Golfers

The **Ai Smoke MAX** wins this comparison because the current official Callaway price is too good to ignore.

- **Ai Smart Face**
- a forgiving stretched-back MAX profile
- **adjustable perimeter weighting**
- Callaway's official **up to 19 yards of shot-shape correction** claim

That is still a real product.

It is a discounted premium head that still solves the actual buying problem most golfers have:

**how do I get more forgiveness without lighting seven hundred bucks on fire?**

That is why the Ai Smoke MAX remains such a strong value branch in the driver cluster. ## Why the GTS2 Still Has a Real Case

The **GTS2** is the more expensive answer for real reasons.

Titleist's current product page positions GTS2 for golfers looking for **added power and consistency across the face**, with **maximum forgiveness** made faster by distributing weight to amplify **speed and stability** at the same time.

The related **May 14, 2026** launch release adds the broader story:

- a new **Split Mass Frame**
- a full thermoform body
- updated face performance
- more adjustability than the previous-generation mainstream Titleist head

That gives the GTS2 a cleaner "current flagship" pitch than the older GT2 lane had.

It is also why golfers who hate overt MAX-head energy may still prefer it. The GTS2 feels like the premium-neutral answer for people who want forgiveness without wanting the club to look like it is doing therapy homework for their miss pattern.

That matters.
