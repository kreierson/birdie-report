## Quick Verdict

Buy the **Players 4 Carbon** if you want the lighter true-carry bag and are willing to accept fewer pockets and less weather insurance to get it.

Buy the **Players S4 StaDry** if you want the smarter premium stand-bag purchase for most golfers.

That is the clean split.

The **Players 4 Carbon** is the more specialized bag.

The **Players S4 StaDry** is the more complete bag.

And because the price gap is only **$30**, the completeness argument matters a lot.

## Why the Players S4 StaDry Gets the Recommendation for Most Golfers

The **Players S4 StaDry** wins this page because it solves more normal-golfer problems for only a little more money.

Titleist's official 2026 S4 StaDry release gives it:

- **StaDry waterproof construction with seam-sealed zippers**
- a **new 4-way top cuff**
- **updated straps**
- a redesigned stand system
- **full-length dividers**
- a **magnetic tee pocket**
- a **cart strap tunnel**
- a **waterproof valuables pocket**
- **8 pockets**

That is a real premium-bag feature stack.

And again, it is only **$30 more** than the Players 4 Carbon at current official pricing.

That changes the argument fast.

If the gap were a hundred bucks, the lighter bag might win more often. At thirty bucks, a lot of golfers are better off taking the extra storage and weather insurance.
