## Quick Verdict

Buy the **Garmin Approach S70** if golf is the main reason you are shopping.

Buy the **Samsung Galaxy Watch Ultra** if you already want Samsung's flagship Android smartwatch anyway, and you just need golf to be good enough through third-party apps.

For most golfers making this exact search before spending real money, I would recommend the **Garmin Approach S70** first.

The **Samsung** only becomes the better answer if the broader Android-smartwatch life matters more to you than the golf part.

## Why the S70 Wins the Golf Argument

The **Garmin Approach S70** is still the cleanest answer in golf-watch shopping because the whole product is built around golf first.

You are not adapting it into a golf tool.

It already gives you:

- **Virtual Caddie**
- **PlaysLike distance**
- detailed course mapping
- hazard and layup context
- green-view tools
- a stronger no-compromise on-course workflow

That is why the full [Garmin Approach S70 review](/blog/garmin-approach-s70-review/) lands so hard. When golfers ask for the best golf watch, this is still the obvious answer.

If your whole reason for buying a watch is:

"I want better on-course decisions from my wrist"

the **S70** is the better product.

## Why Samsung Still Has a Real Case

Because some golfers do not really want a golf watch.

They want a **premium Android smartwatch** that can also cover golf, workouts, notifications, and normal life without feeling like a dedicated golf gadget.

That is the **Galaxy Watch Ultra** lane.

Samsung wins the everyday-watch argument with:

- broader app life
- better general Android integration
- more normal flagship-smartwatch identity
- less "I am wearing a golf device to dinner" energy

That matters. It just does not make Samsung the better golf product.

## The Golf-App Dependency Is the Whole Samsung Problem

This is the deciding issue.

The **Galaxy Watch Ultra** is not a golf-first watch with a native built-in golf product identity.

The golf side still depends on **third-party apps**.

That means:

- extra setup
- extra app decisions
- less consistent golf workflow
- less confidence that the golf part is the whole point of the watch

If you already wanted Samsung anyway, that compromise may be fine.

If you are buying because of golf, it is a weaker answer immediately.

That is why the more natural Samsung comparison for value-minded buyers is still [Samsung Galaxy Watch Ultra vs Garmin Approach S50](/blog/samsung-galaxy-watch-ultra-vs-garmin-approach-s50/). But if you are comparing Samsung to Garmin's best, the golf gap gets wider, not narrower.

## Battery and One-Watch Life

Samsung absolutely has a case here.

The **Galaxy Watch Ultra** is easier to justify if your watch also needs to cover:

- workday notifications
- health tracking
- workouts
- daily smartwatch use
- Android ecosystem life

The **S70** still works beyond golf. The full [S70 review](/blog/garmin-approach-s70-review/) makes that clear. It is a better everyday wearable than older golf watches were.

But the order of priorities is still different:

- **Samsung:** smartwatch first, golf second
- **Garmin S70:** golf first, smartwatch second

That difference matters more than a lot of spec-sheet comparisons admit.

## Price: Closer Than It Looks, Still Favoring Garmin for Golf

At first glance Samsung looks cheaper because the **Galaxy Watch Ultra** starts at **$499.99 before trade-in**, while the **S70** begins at **$600** and goes to **$700** for the bigger 47mm version.

But once you admit the S70 is delivering the better golf product out of the box, the price gap stops looking outrageous.

Samsung is asking you to spend premium-smartwatch money on a device that still needs third-party golf help.

Garmin is asking you to spend premium-golf-watch money on the best golf watch in the category.

Those are different value propositions.

## Final Verdict

The **Samsung Galaxy Watch Ultra** is the better **Android flagship smartwatch**.

The **Garmin Approach S70** is the better **golf watch**, and it is not especially close.

If your shopping starts with golf, buy the **S70**.

If your shopping starts with Samsung and you just need golf to be good enough, the **Galaxy Watch Ultra** still has a real case.
