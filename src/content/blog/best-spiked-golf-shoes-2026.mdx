## How to Decide If You Actually Need Spikes

Buy spiked shoes if:

- you play wet morning golf a lot
- your courses are hilly enough that traction anxiety is a real thing
- you like a more planted, structured shoe through the swing
- you are willing to trade some casual comfort for more grip confidence

Skip spiked shoes if:

- you mostly walk dry courses
- you want one pair that is easier to wear before and after the round
- you prioritize lighter weight and more natural flex
- you are mostly buying spikes because they feel more "serious"

That last reason is how golfers end up with gear they admire more than they enjoy.

## Final Verdict

The **adidas Tour360 24** is the best spiked golf shoe in 2026 for most golfers who truly want spikes.

The **FootJoy Traditions** are the smarter value buy for golfers who want classic styling and dependable traction without paying premium-performance prices.

The **FootJoy Premiere Series Packard** is the luxury classic answer.

And the **FootJoy Pro SL 2026** is the shoe many spike shoppers should probably buy instead.

That is the market right now. Fewer golfers need spikes than brands would like. The golfers who do still have a few very good options.
